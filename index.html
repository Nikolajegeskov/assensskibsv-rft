About us

Assens Shipyard has proud traditions of building and repairing ships of all kinds. We offer the best personalized service which we deliver with a smile. We have a long history, but today we are a modern shipyard who are environmentally oriented and concerned about our planet. Our relatively small size is an asset as it allows us to work more efficiently and keep in touch with all our current employees and projects. We have 5-6 administrators who manage all our daily activities and tasks. We are focused on safety as a priority, as we care about all our employees.

Technical Difficulties?


If you are encountering any technical difficulties at sea, feel free to contact us and we will gladly provide the service needed to get you back on course.
