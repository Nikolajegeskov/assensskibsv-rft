Conversion

Assens Shipyard performs all kinds of services in ship repairs. We have many years of experience with ferries, tugs, fishing boats, etc. We have 2 dry docks where one of the docks is covered, giving us optimal conditions for well done work. We take in all vessels that fit our capacity. We have a good working environment, also with regard to all types of operation and secure access conditions at the quay. We are a workplace where there is not far from the workers to the management. We always put services and high quality first.
