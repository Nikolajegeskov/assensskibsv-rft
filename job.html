Our vision

We are a dependable and modern shipyard located in Assens, Denmark. here we look forward, with focus on being environmentally conscience, and having a safe and healthy work environment. we adhere to all of Denmark's health and safety laws, not just because we need to but because we want the best for our employees, you are not going to see someone in the shipyard without a hardhat on. we also care about our community and have upgrade our fasciitis to insure the we don’t disturb the people around us. it is also important for us to welcome the new generation, we are happy to take young people under our wing and teach them our craft. we here at Assens shipyard are ready to take on the future.
