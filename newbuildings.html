Newbuilding

One of the types new building we do alot is ferries. Our yard delivered over the years several ferries with a variety of specifications - all according to the geographical and physical conditions required. See also our our reference lists for new buildings Our yard also delivered this special cutter build expedition steel touring vessels ice-strengthened. Carl Bro Industry made the Marine Design and needed calculations - And we made the vessel. we also think a lot about the environment, therefore energy optimisation is something we are doing alot on our newbuildings as this will save environment and, you money in the long run. we will build what you need. you can always call os if you have questions about your specific newbuildings you want os to do. See also our reference lists for new buildings. All our newbuildings and buildings procedures are well documented.
