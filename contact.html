Where to find us

Assens Shipyard is a service/production company with a lot of experience in repairs, newbuildings and redevelopments of ferries, trawlers and other types of commercial vessels. Assens Skibsværft A/S is centrally located next to the Little Belt in Denmark and the sailing distance is short from all positions in the Baltics, Kattegat and Skagerak.
